These visualizations are intended to provide a proof of concept in order to generate more questions and direction for a broader project.

The following holds true for the visualizations:

  • Data is that of Routing Forms and Awards Processed into Sponsored Programs between 1/1/2011 and 9/10/2014.
  • Investigators are connected if they appeared on the same grant together as Principal Investigator, Co-Investigator, or Multiple Investigators.
  • Filtering by a college will display individuals in that college and the individuals they occur with on Routing forms, even if they are in another college.
  • Date filters will display those individuals who's first collaboration happened between the start and end date.
  • Matrix cells and links on the Chord Diagram and Force Directed Graph indicate the scale of the awarded grants when awarded.
